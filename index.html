Flido: A mobility-assistive robot with world model

For individuals with low vision, powered by a world model to avoid obstacles and reach destinations.

FLIDO - Redefining Mobility for the Visually Impaired

Flido is a mobility-assistive robot designed for individuals with low vision. Powered by our proprietary end-to-end navigation model, it identifies navigable areas, avoids obstacles intelligently, and guides users to destinations like elevators, markets, and parks. With Flido, individuals with low vision can confidently step outside and explore the world freely.

End-to-End Navigation Model

We have developed the most powerful end-to-end navigation model to date. With just voice and image inputs, the model directly generates motor commands, making it the "ChatGPT" of the robotics world. Trained on over 10,000 hours of navigation data, the model has acquired robust navigation knowledge. It understands navigable areas and has a strong sense of obstacle avoidance.

Navigation

Flido supports three guiding modes:

Exploratory Navigation: Designed for unfamiliar environments, helping users adapt quickly and travel safely.

Long-Distance Navigation: Supports navigation over distances of up to tens of kilometers, ideal for long journeys.

Memory Navigation: Tailored for frequently visited places like shopping malls and office buildings, offering precise and reliable guidance.

Hardware Specifications

AI Performance: 100 TOPS

GPU: 1024-core NVIDIA Ampere architecture GPU with 32 Tensor Cores

GPU Max Frequency: 918 MHz

CPU: 8-core Arm® Cortex

CPU Max Frequency: 2 GHz

Motor Reduction Ratio: 7.75:1

Motor Peak Torque: 12N·m

High-Decibel Speaker: Ultra-loud volume ranging from 100-110 dB.

Buttons: Includes power and selection buttons.

Bluetooth: Connects to a mobile app via Bluetooth.

Handle: Retractable design for user convenience.

Vibration Haptic Feedback Sensor (Handle): Encodes 200 unique vibration patterns, each representing an obstacle or special scenario.

Weight: Less than 2.9 kg.

Rechargeable Battery: Supports all-day charging and provides over 5.5 hours of continuous use.

Waterproof Design: Resistant to rain and splashes.
